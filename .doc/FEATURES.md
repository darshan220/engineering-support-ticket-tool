# Key Features

The **Engineering Support Ticket Tool** is packed with features designed to optimize the ticket management lifecycle. Below is a detailed breakdown of the primary functionalities.

## 1. Authentication & Security
-   **Secure Onboarding**: Robust Sign Up and Sign In flows to ensure secure access.
-   **Role-Based Access**: Foundational support for different user roles (Support, Engineer, Admin).
-   **Profile Management**: Personalized settings for users to manage their identity within the tool.

## 2. Advanced Kanban Board
-   **Visual Workflow**: Drag-and-drop interface for moving tickets through status columns (e.g., Backlog, In Progress, Review, Done).
-   **Sorting & Filtering**: Ability to organize tickets by priority, assignee, or team.
-   **Real-time Updates**: Visual indicators when ticket data changes.

## 3. Comprehensive Ticket Management
-   **Rich Ticket Details**: Detailed views for each ticket including descriptions, priority levels, and category tags.
-   **Creation & Editing**: Streamlined forms for creating new issues and updating existing ones.
-   **Assignment System**: Easily assign tickets to specific engineering teams or individual members.

## 4. Team & Member Management
-   **Team Creation**: Define specific engineering teams (e.g., Frontend, Backend, DevOps).
-   **Member Directory**: Manage team members, roles, and expertise levels.
-   **Collaborative Spaces**: Filter views and boards specifically for team-level tracking.

## 5. Analytics & Dashboard
-   **Performance Metrics**: Track KPIs such as average resolution time and ticket throughput.
-   **Visual Charts**: Integrated Recharts for visualizing ticket distribution by priority and team performance trends.
-   **Executive Summary**: A high-level overview of the system health and active workloads.

## 6. Smart Notifications
-   **Activity Stream**: Stay updated on recent changes, assignments, and comments.
-   **Filtering**: Manage which updates are most relevant to your workflow.
-   **Real-time Alerts**: In-app notifications for critical status changes.

## 7. Modern UI/UX
-   **Dark/Light Mode**: Seamless theme switching to suit user preference and environment.
-   **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for various screen sizes, from large monitors to tablets.
-   **Glassmorphic Aesthetics**: A premium look and feel with smooth animations and subtle transitions.
